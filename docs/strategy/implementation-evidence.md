## Production evidence location

Sanitized staging and production acceptance JSON belongs under `docs/operations/evidence/<release-id>/`. Dashboard URLs may be recorded in the ledger, but credentials and tokens must never be committed. Required artifacts are defined in `docs/operations/realtime-acceptance.md` and generated by the guarded realtime acceptance harness where applicable.
